What is prompt.fail?

Prompt.fail is a celebration of the gap between human intention and AI interpretation. We showcase the most interesting, unexpected, and humorous AI responses that completely miss the mark.

From bizarre interpretations to complete non-sequiturs, we showcase the fascinating world of prompt failures as a way to better understand the limitations and creative potential of AI systems.
